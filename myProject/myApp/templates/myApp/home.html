SERVICES

Radiant Transition Coaching

A specific coaching path for women entering or navigating perimenopause and menopause. Not just "managing symptoms" but embracing menopause as a new season of power, wisdom, and radiance. Outcomes: Feeling supported, understood, and empowered in this transition.

Radiance Coaching (Private Sessions)

Personalized coaching sessions designed around your unique journey. We work together to clear emotional weight, rebuild self-trust, and help you feel rooted in your truth. Along the way, you'll explore practices that nurture balance, vitality, and clarity so you can move forward feeling lighter and stronger.

Emotional Healing & Inner Peace

For women carrying years of unspoken stress, suppressed emotions, and long-lived responsibilities. A safe, guided space to process emotions, patterns, and stories that keep you stuck. Outcomes: Emotional clarity, a lighter heart, more inner calm, and self-awareness.

Group Coaching Circles

Small, safe, healing spaces where women come together. These intimate, women-only circles allow you to share, be witnessed, and grow together. Themes include identity shifts, self-worth, aging, caregiving, transitions, and boundary-setting. Outcomes: Community, support, and the feeling of "I'm not alone in this."

Life Direction & Purpose Mapping

A structured program for women asking: "What's next for me?" "What do I want now?" "Who am I becoming in this season?" We'll explore clarifying values, strengths, desires, and a vision for your next chapter. Outcomes: New sense of direction, renewed identity, and practical next steps.

Mindfulness & Emotional Regulation

Sessions focused on practical tools: breathwork, grounding, self-regulation, and nervous system support. Perfect for women experiencing hormonal shifts, burnout, insomnia, or emotional ups and downs. Outcomes: More calm, emotional stability, and the ability to handle life's chaos with more grace.
